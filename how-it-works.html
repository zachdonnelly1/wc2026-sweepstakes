// THE SETUP

💰
€15 ENTRY · 16 PLAYERS · €240 POT
Everyone pays in, one draw decides your fate
Each of the 16 players puts in €15. The full €240 pot is paid out across 6 prizes during and after the tournament — no house cut.
🎰
THE DRAW
3 teams each, one from every tier
The 48 World Cup teams are split into 3 tiers of 16. Each player draws one team per tier — you end up with 3 teams representing you in the tournament.
TIER 1 — STRONG
The favourites. Argentina, France, Brazil...
TIER 2 — MID
Dark horses. Japan, Norway, Switzerland...
TIER 3 — UNDERDOG
The dreamers. Haiti, Iraq, Curaçao...

// THE PRIZES — THE AVALANCHE

SEMI-FINAL SPLIT

// HOW TO FOLLOW ALONG

01
Check the leaderboard — it updates automatically every 5 minutes from live match data. Your 3 teams are tracked every step of the way.
02
Watch the team chips — green means your team is still alive, grey with strikethrough means they're out. Prizes lock in as results come in.
03
Follow the prize projections — the leaderboard shows who's currently tracking for Underdog Hero, Beautiful Loser and Wooden Spoon in real time.
04
Share on WhatsApp — hit the Share button on the leaderboard to send a text snapshot of the current standings to the group.
